This semester (Fall 2021) we are running a seminar on category theory for graduate students at Wayne State University. In particular, the seminar will focus on ∞-categories. It will be held on Fridays at 1:30 p.m. in RM 1258 in the Math Department. Below are a list of references for the seminar and a tentative schedule of talks.

Category Theory in Context - Emily Riehl
A short course on ∞-categories - Moritz Groth
